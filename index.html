About

Nidhi Jain School of Fine Arts is a private organisation, which targets in comprehensive development of the students in the field of fine arts.

We're a team of self-starting, mission-driven individuals with a passion for enriching others knowledge in the best way possible. We value what makes you different, and encourage you to join us in this wonderful jounrey ahead.

Our aim is to make students explore their true inner-self using the language of art.
